# Future Roadmap

## Booking

Add self-service booking for workshops, sessions, and retreats with capacity, waitlists, confirmation emails, and calendar integration.

## Payments

Add payments for workshops, courses, meditations, gift cards, and shop items. Stripe is the likely provider.

## Gift Cards

Support digital gift cards with custom message, value, expiry, and redemption tracking.

## Newsletter

Add segmented newsletters by language, interest, and signup source. Integrate with a simple email tool or custom Supabase list.

## Podcast

Create podcast pages with episodes, show notes, transcripts, embedded players, and related articles or meditations.

## Client Login

Allow clients to access purchased courses, private resources, booking history, and saved meditations.

## Video Library

Support paid and free videos with categories, duration, transcripts, and progress tracking.

## Online Courses

Expand the course model with modules, lessons, comments, progress, certificates, and drip scheduling.

## Meditation Library

Build a searchable audio library with themes, durations, favorites, recently played, and offline-friendly downloads if appropriate.

## Retreat Booking

Support retreat pages with rooms, schedules, deposits, payment plans, travel details, and participant forms.

## Shop

Sell physical or digital products such as journals, cards, audio bundles, or retreat materials.

## Community

Add a gentle private community space with discussion prompts, group announcements, and course cohorts.
