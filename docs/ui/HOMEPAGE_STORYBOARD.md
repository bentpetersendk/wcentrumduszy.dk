# Homepage Storyboard

## 1. Arrival Hero

Purpose: establish calm, Joanna, and the brand promise.

Visual appearance: warm paper background, large portrait, minimal navigation, one strong sentence.

Typography: display serif title, body sans subtitle.

Photography: `joanna-radek-01.png` or clean equivalent.

Animations: slow fade on text, delayed image reveal.

Expected emotion: "I can slow down here."

CTA: `Explore workshops` and `Read about Joanna`.

Estimated scroll duration: 8-12 seconds.

## 2. Trust Statement

Purpose: translate emotion into grounded clarity.

Visual appearance: centered reading-width text with one small logo-derived line detail.

Typography: large serif statement plus short sans paragraph.

Photography: none or very subtle close-up.

Animations: text fades into place.

Expected emotion: "This is thoughtful, not exaggerated."

CTA: none or quiet `How Joanna works`.

Estimated scroll duration: 6-10 seconds.

## 3. Offer Pathways

Purpose: show ways to begin without pressure.

Visual appearance: open four-column desktop layout, one card each for Workshops, Courses, Meditations, Articles.

Typography: compact headings, practical summaries.

Photography: small disciplined image crops, not icons as decoration.

Animations: staggered reveal, subtle hover border.

Expected emotion: "I can choose my pace."

CTA: `View workshops`, `Explore courses`, `Listen`, `Read`.

Estimated scroll duration: 12-18 seconds.

## 4. About Joanna Preview

Purpose: deepen trust through presence.

Visual appearance: asymmetric portrait and text layout.

Typography: warm H2, body copy with restrained link.

Photography: `joanna-radek-11.png` or clean equivalent.

Animations: image settles upward 12px; text fades.

Expected emotion: "Joanna feels real."

CTA: `Read about Joanna`.

Estimated scroll duration: 10-16 seconds.

## 5. Featured Workshop Or Course

Purpose: make the next step concrete.

Visual appearance: full-width soft mist band with offer details and practical metadata.

Typography: title with clear date/location metadata.

Photography: `joanna-radek-13.png` for table/workshop preparation if approved.

Animations: metadata appears after heading.

Expected emotion: "I understand what I could join."

CTA: `View details` or `Ask a question`.

Estimated scroll duration: 14-20 seconds.

## 6. Meditation Or Article Sample

Purpose: offer a low-risk first experience.

Visual appearance: quiet editorial split: short text, audio/article preview, image detail.

Typography: soft H2, small metadata.

Photography: use calm portrait/detail; replace with clean nature/detail photography later.

Animations: progress/play control hover only; no auto-playing media.

Expected emotion: "I can try something small."

CTA: `Listen now` or `Read the article`.

Estimated scroll duration: 10-16 seconds.

## 7. Testimonials

Purpose: reduce perceived risk.

Visual appearance: one featured quote, not a carousel by default.

Typography: large quote serif, small attribution.

Photography: optional subtle background texture, no client faces unless consented.

Animations: quote fades in.

Expected emotion: "Others have felt safe here."

CTA: `Read more stories`.

Estimated scroll duration: 8-12 seconds.

## 8. Newsletter

Purpose: allow connection without commitment.

Visual appearance: compact form on warm surface, no large sales framing.

Typography: clear heading, one sentence of copy.

Photography: optional small image of notebook or Joanna writing.

Animations: input focus and success transition.

Expected emotion: "I can stay close without deciding."

CTA: `Join the newsletter`.

Estimated scroll duration: 8-12 seconds.

## 9. Contact Closing

Purpose: provide final clarity and action.

Visual appearance: warm portrait, short message, contact button, footer preview.

Typography: calm closing statement.

Photography: `joanna-radek-17.png` or clean equivalent.

Animations: none beyond standard reveal.

Expected emotion: "I know how to begin."

CTA: `Contact Joanna`.

Estimated scroll duration: 8-12 seconds.
