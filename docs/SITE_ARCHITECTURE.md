# Site Architecture

## Primary Navigation

- Home
- About Joanna
- Workshops
- Courses
- Meditations
- Articles
- Gallery
- Contact

Secondary navigation:

- FAQ
- Testimonials
- Newsletter
- Privacy Policy
- Terms

## Homepage Sections

1. Hero: Joanna, brand promise, primary CTA, secondary CTA.
2. Trust introduction: short text explaining the work.
3. Offer pathways: workshops, courses, meditations, articles.
4. About Joanna preview.
5. Featured workshop or course.
6. Featured meditation or article.
7. Testimonials.
8. Newsletter invitation.
9. Contact invitation.

## About Page

- Hero portrait.
- Joanna's story.
- Approach and values.
- Qualifications or experience.
- What clients can expect.
- Personal note.
- CTA to contact or explore offers.

## Workshops

Listing:

- Featured workshop.
- Upcoming workshops.
- Past workshops archive.
- Filters by theme, date, format, language, location.

Detail:

- Title, subtitle, date, location, language, format.
- Description, outcomes, agenda, who it is for, practical details.
- Price and booking status.
- Testimonials or photos.
- FAQ and CTA.

## Courses

Courses can be live, self-paced, or hybrid. Each course needs modules, lessons, enrollment state, and future payment support.

## Meditations

Meditation library should support audio, video, duration, theme, language, free/paid access, and related articles or courses.

## Articles

Editorial content for SEO and trust. Categories should include personal growth, calm, presence, self-reflection, workshops, and Joanna's notes.

## Gallery

Curated image collections for portraits, spaces, workshops, nature, and brand atmosphere.

## FAQ

Reusable FAQ items can appear globally or on specific workshops, courses, and contact pages.

## Contact

- Calm form with reason selector.
- Direct email and social links.
- Optional phone or location if approved.
- Consent and privacy notice.

## Footer

- Logo and short brand statement.
- Primary links.
- Contact details.
- Newsletter signup.
- Social links.
- Legal links.
- Language selector.

## Future Expansion

The structure supports booking, checkout, gift cards, retreats, shop, podcast, community, and client login without changing the core navigation model.
