# Component Library

## Principles

Components should be calm, refined, accessible, and content-driven. They should support CMS content and multilingual text without layout breakage.

## Navigation

- Desktop header with logo, primary links, language switcher, and contact CTA.
- Mobile drawer with large tap targets.
- Sticky behavior should be subtle and never cover content.

## Hero

Variants:

- Homepage editorial hero with portrait or calm brand image.
- Offer hero with practical metadata.
- Article hero with title, excerpt, category, and reading time.
- Minimal legal/content hero.

## Buttons

- Primary: quiet clay or charcoal fill.
- Secondary: text or outlined button.
- Tertiary: inline link.
- All buttons need hover, focus, disabled, loading, and icon-capable states.

## Cards

- Offer card.
- Workshop card.
- Course card.
- Meditation card.
- Article card.
- Testimonial card.
- Gallery card.

Cards should use 8px radius or less, clear hierarchy, and stable image ratios.

## Forms

- Contact form.
- Newsletter form.
- Booking interest form.
- Admin content forms.

States: default, focused, completed, invalid, disabled, loading, success, error.

## Footer

Footer includes logo, short description, navigation, newsletter, social links, legal links, and language selector.

## FAQ

Accordion component with keyboard support, deep-link support, and optional category grouping.

## Gallery

- Masonry or calm grid view.
- Lightbox for larger inspection.
- Captions and alt text.
- Mobile swipe support.

## Testimonials

- Single featured quote.
- Testimonial grid.
- Related testimonial block on offers.

## Animations

- Page entrance fade.
- Section reveal.
- Image soft scale on hover.
- Accordion open/close.
- Loading skeleton.
- All motion must respect reduced-motion settings.

## Empty States

Empty states should be plain and helpful:

- No upcoming workshops.
- No articles in this language.
- No gallery images yet.
- No search results.

## Loading States

Use lightweight skeletons for cards and forms. Avoid spinners where content structure is known.

## Error States

Errors should be calm, specific, and actionable. Form errors should appear near the relevant field and in a summary for screen readers.

## Responsive Behaviour

- Mobile first.
- Navigation collapses below tablet width.
- Cards move from 3 columns to 2 to 1.
- Images maintain stable aspect ratios.
- Text must wrap naturally in Polish, Danish, and English.
