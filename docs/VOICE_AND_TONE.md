# Voice And Tone

## Purpose

This document extends `COPYWRITING_GUIDE.md`, `BRAND_STORY.md`, and `CONTENT_MODEL.md`. It defines exactly how Joanna sounds across the website, CMS content, emails, forms, SEO snippets, and social previews.

## Core Voice

Joanna's voice is calm, clear, warm, and grounded. It should sound like a thoughtful person speaking with care, not like a marketing funnel, therapist diagnosis, or spiritual performance.

## Tone Principles

- Use plain language for deep subjects.
- Stay specific without becoming clinical.
- Invite rather than persuade.
- Describe possibilities, not guaranteed outcomes.
- Respect the visitor's pace.
- Keep warmth adult and steady.

## Sentence Length

Use mostly short and medium sentences. Longer sentences are acceptable when they create rhythm, but they should remain easy to read.

Recommended rhythm:

```text
Short sentence. Medium sentence with one clear idea. A slightly longer sentence can follow when it creates softness and context.
```

## Good Writing

"A quiet space to notice what has been asking for your attention."

"Explore patterns that may still influence your life, relationships, and choices."

"You are welcome to begin gently. There is no need to have everything figured out."

"Workshops are small, reflective, and guided with care."

"This practice supports calm attention and a more compassionate relationship with yourself."

## Poor Writing

"Heal your trauma and become your highest self."

"Unlock your soul's secret power."

"Transform your entire life in one weekend."

"Book now before your old patterns destroy your future."

"This method guarantees emotional freedom."

## Warmth

Warmth should come from permission, care, and specificity. Avoid excessive reassurance or sentimental language.

Good:

"You can arrive exactly as you are."

Too soft:

"You are a beautiful soul and everything is perfect."

## Professionalism

Professionalism should come from clarity, structure, and honest boundaries. Joanna can be warm without sounding vague.

Good:

"Each workshop includes guided reflection, quiet exercises, group conversation, and time for integration."

Too vague:

"We will enter a sacred field and let the process unfold."

## CTA Tone

Use CTAs that feel like choices:

- Contact Joanna
- Explore workshops
- Read about Joanna
- Listen to a meditation
- Join the newsletter
- Ask a question
- Save my place

Avoid:

- Transform now
- Claim your breakthrough
- Start healing today
- Don't miss your chance
