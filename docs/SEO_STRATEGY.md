# SEO Strategy

## Purpose

This five-year strategy extends `CONTENT_MODEL.md` and `TECHNICAL_ARCHITECTURE.md`. SEO should grow from authentic expertise, not content farming.

## SEO Principles

- Write for real people first.
- Build topical authority around calm, presence, personal growth, relationships, workshops, meditations, and Joanna's name.
- Support Polish first, then Danish and English.
- Use internal links to guide readers toward practical next steps.
- Avoid medical claims unless properly qualified.

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every article should link to:

- one related article
- one relevant meditation
- one workshop or course when appropriate
- About Joanna when trust context matters
- newsletter when the visitor may not be ready for a paid offer

Every workshop should link to:

- related articles
- FAQ
- Joanna's About page
- contact form

Every meditation should link to:

- related article
- related workshop or course
- newsletter

## Five-Year Growth Plan

Year 1: establish brand, core pages, foundational articles, basic meditations.

Year 2: expand workshop topic clusters and multilingual SEO.

Year 3: build course SEO, evergreen meditation library, and newsletter archive.

Year 4: add retreats, podcast transcripts, video content, and structured data depth.

Year 5: build authority through collaborations, interviews, community resources, and long-form guides.
